tueSday

TueSday is a platform that provides standard operating procedures with a variety of features exclusively for Marketers.

The challenge

In every field, especially Marketing, people have to work with way too many papers and platforms. This costs a lot of time, which can be better used to optimize productivities. Furthermore, not everyone knows the best process to perform their projects. A solution that is able to conquer all these problems is highly demanded to enhance productivity and life quality of workers in the industry.

The outcome

tueSday realized the urgent need of a comprehensive solution across the market. Hence, we developed tueSday platform for better project management and easier working standardization, with the core of SOPs (Standard Operation Procedures).
