Urban Oasis

Urban Oasis is the redesigned website of The Dutch Store - a premier grocery store offering a wide variety of authentic Dutch products in the U.S. From delicious cheeses, traditional snacks and beverages to wooden shoes and household items, they bring a piece of the Netherlands to their customers. With a strong commitment to quality and customer satisfaction, The Dutch Store has become a go-to destination for Dutch expatriates, enthusiasts of Dutch cuisine, and those seeking a unique culinary experience.

The challenge

The current website of The Dutch Store is outdated and impractical with a lack of responsiveness, confusing navigation, and buried information. There is a strong need to redesign the website to meet the modern web standards and enhance the purchase experience of the users.

The outcome

Urban Oasis, The Dutch Store's redesigned website, brings a breath of fresh air with simplified navigation and personalized suggestions, ensuring a delightful and user-friendly shopping experience. Discover authentic Dutch products effortlessly, enjoy secure checkouts, and experience responsive support, all thoughtfully designed to elevate the online journey of a customer.
